About PUCC

Premier University Computer Club (PUCC) is the flagship technical student organization at Premier University. Founded in 2015, we bring together students passionate about computing, innovation, and collaboration. Our mission is to foster a culture of excellence in software development, competitive programming, and emerging technologies.

With 600+ active members and 6 specialized wings, we provide a platform for learning, building, and competing at national and international levels.

Our Journey

2015 · Founding

PUCC was established with a vision to create a community of passionate technologists at Premier University.

2017 · First Hackathon

Hosted the inaugural inter-university hackathon, attracting 50+ participants from across the region.

2020 · Official Approval

PUCC received official university recognition, solidifying its role as a premier student organization.

2025 · 6 Wings Established

Expanded to six specialized wings, covering every facet of modern computing and technology.

Vision

To be the leading university tech club that produces world-class technologists and innovators.

Explore Our Wings

Detailed overview of each specialized domain.

Competitive Programming

Intensive problem-solving training, weekly contests, and preparation for ICPC, IOI, and national olympiads.

  • Weekly practice sessions
  • Mock contests
  • National team selection

Software Engineering

Full-stack development, modern frameworks, and deployment pipelines. Real-world projects with industry partners.

  • Project-based learning
  • Industry mentorship
  • Hackathon sprints

Network & Cybersecurity

Ethical hacking, CTF challenges, network defense, and security research. Hands-on labs and simulation.

  • Linux administration
  • Security bootcamps
  • Capture The Flag events

Deep Neural Research

Deep learning, computer vision, NLP, and generative AI. Research-driven projects and Kaggle participation.

  • Research paper reviews
  • Model building workshops
  • Kaggle competitions

Research & Development

3D modeling, game engines, VR/AR, and interactive media. Creative and technical fusion.

  • Prototyping labs
  • Innovation challenges
  • Cross-disciplinary projects

Career Guidance

  • Career fairs
  • Alumni networking
  • Resume & interview prep
